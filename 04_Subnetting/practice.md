## Question 1

Network:

```text
192.168.1.0/24
```

Find:
- Subnet mask
- Broadcast address
- Usable hosts

Answer:

```text
Subnet mask: 255.255.255.0
Broadcast address: 192.168.1.255
Usable hosts: 192.168.1.1 - 192.168.1.254
```
