# Day 2 - Types of Networks

## What is a Network?

A network is a connection between two or more devices to share data and resources.

Examples:
- Internet
- Wi-Fi
- Bluetooth
- Office networks

---

# Types of Networks

## 1. PAN (Personal Area Network)

A small network used for personal devices.

Examples:
- Bluetooth earbuds
- Smart watch connection
- Mobile hotspot

Range:
Very short distance

---

## 2. LAN (Local Area Network)

A network inside a small area like:
- Home
- School
- Office

Examples:
- Home Wi-Fi
- Computer lab network

Advantages:
- Fast speed
- Private connection

---

## 3. MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

A network covering a city or large area.

Examples:
- City-wide internet systems
- Cable TV networks

Range:
Larger than LAN

---

## 4. WAN (Wide Area Network)

A network covering countries or the entire world.

Example:
- Internet

Advantages:
- Global communication
- Connects multiple LANs

---

# Summary

PAN < LAN < MAN < WAN

Network size increases from PAN to WAN.
